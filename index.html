How to use this log

  1. After the NYC DOL monthly jobs report (~3rd Friday of month), record the new unemployment rate.
  2. After StreetEasy or Redfin monthly update, record Manhattan median price and rental figure.
  3. After the NYC Comptroller Monthly Newsletter, note any city finance changes.
  4. After NYPD CompStat weekly update, flag any significant crime trend shift.
  5. If 3+ dashboard signals turn red simultaneously, note it here with intended response.
  6. Review before any muni bond or portfolio decisions — NYC fiscal health directly affects NYC bond prices.
